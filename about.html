About this website

This website uses artificial intelligence to give an estimation of your beauty level. Just with an algorithm and a picture, you can receive a score in seconds. Please bear in mind that, like humans, algorithm is prone to bias. Two different people may have a completely different beauty perception, and the same happens with this algorithm. Therefore, use this website just for fun and do not let it affect your self-steem. No images are saved during the process, so your privacy is always protected.
